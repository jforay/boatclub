Membership Perks

Its All About The Perks

On a first come, first served basis, Destination Boat Clubs Carolinas™ offers special perks just by being a member.

Charlotte Checkers

Destination Boat Clubs Carolinas™ has two tickets for the Charlotte Checkers Home Games in the lower bowl. They are free to members on a first come, first served basis.

Carolina Panthers

Destination Boat Clubs Carolinas™ has tickets for the Carolina Panthers Home Games. They are free to members on a first come, first served basis.

Kannapolis Cannon Ballers

Destination Boat Clubs Carolinas™ is proud to sponser the Kannapolis Cannon Ballers Baseball. We offer tickets to home games to members. They are free to members on a first come, first served basis.

Coca Cola 600

Destination Boat Clubs Carolinas™ has tickets to the Coca Cola 600. They are free to members on a first come, first served basis. Call for availability.

Charlotte Knights

Destination Boat Clubs Carolinas™ is proud to sponser Charlotte Knights Baseball. We offer tickets to home games to members. They are free to members on a first come, first served basis.

Beach House

Destination Boat Clubs Carolinas™ has a beach house available for its members only in Outer Banks near Beaufort, NC.

Call for availability.

Tiny Lake House

Destination Boat Clubs Carolinas™ has a lake house available on Lake James, North Carolina that is available for use.

Call for availability.
